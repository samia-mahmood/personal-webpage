Aside from my research, I enjoy expressing my creativity through art. It provides a refreshing contrast to the analytical nature of scientific work and allows me to engage with ideas in a more intuitive and visual way. Creating art helps me maintain balance and often inspires new ways of thinking in my academic pursuits.

Highlights

April 17 2025: Titled "Timeless Treasures" and painted with oil paint. I had the privilege of having my artwork showcased in the McGrath Art Gallery at Bellarmine Unviersity for Bellarmine's Student Research and Creativity Week. I was honored with the Arts & Craftsmen Award which is presented to undergraduate students who showcased exemplary talent and craftsmenship.
